that cost $1B when wrong.

Phase 3 failures cost $300M–$1B per program. Most could have been signaled earlier. KIP gives your portfolio team the quantified POS, competitive map, and eNPV to defend — or reconsider — a program before the money is committed.

What-If Protocol Optimizer

CI Intelligence
